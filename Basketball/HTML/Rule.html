Basketball Rules & Regulations

Understanding the game: fouls, violations, and official regulations.

Basketball Rulebook

Basketball is governed by a set of rules that ensure fair play and maintain the integrity of the game. The two main categories of infractions in basketball are fouls and violations. Understanding these rules is essential for players, coaches, and fans alike.

Fouls in Basketball

Fouls involve illegal physical contact with an opponent or unsportsmanlike behavior. When a player commits a foul, the opposing team is awarded penalties such as free throws or possession of the ball.

Violations in Basketball

Violations are infractions of the rules that do not involve physical contact with an opponent. When a violation occurs, the opposing team is awarded possession of the ball.
