Basketball History

The Birth of Basketball

Basketball was invented by Dr. James Naismith in December 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts. Originally played with a peach basket and a soccer ball, the sport has evolved dramatically over the past century.

The NBA (National Basketball Association) was founded in 1946 and has grown to become the world's premier basketball league, featuring the greatest players from around the globe.

Today, basketball is played by more than 450 million people worldwide, making it one of the most popular sports on the planet.
